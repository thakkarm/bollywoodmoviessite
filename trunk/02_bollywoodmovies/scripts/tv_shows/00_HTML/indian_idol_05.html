The Judges
In 2004-2005, the 1st edition of the popular Indian idol contest was judged by of Sonu Nigam, Anu Malik and Farah Khan. They again judged the next set of contestants for year 2005-2006, Indian Idol 2 but for season 3 of Indian Idol two new judges joined Anu Malik, and they were Udit Narayan and Alisha Chinoy. In season 4, Udit Narayan and Alisha Chinoy were replaced with Kailash Kher and Sonali Bendre.

Anu Malik
Anu is a well known music director in the Bollywood film industry. He has won many awards including the National Award for his work in Refugee. He has also provided music for such hits as Janeman (2006) , Umrao Jaan (2006) , Zindaggi Rocks (2006) to just mention a few.
Salim Merchant
Salim Merchant is a music director duo and singers. The duo consists of two brothers, Salim Merchant and Sulaiman Merchant. The duo have provided music for success movies like Neal N Nikki (2005) , Chak De India (2007) , Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi (RNBDJ) (2008) and Fashion (2008) to mention a few.

09/23/2007:
It was announced that Prashant Tamang from Darjeeling got the most votes and will be crowned the Indian Idol 3, winner beating his friend Amit Paul for the title of Indian Idol 3. Prashant is Kolkata constable from Darjeeling and received more then seven crore votes. Prashant total the show with his singing of Yeh Kaali Kaali Aankhen from the Hindi film Baazigar. While Amit sang Duma Dum Mast Kalandar. Bollywood actor John Abraham announced Tamang the winner.
Prashant is a good singer but Amit is a much better overall singer and should have won the Indian Idol title. This show has turned into not finding the best Indian Idol but who can get more votes. Prashant had the big backing of his state and his fellow collegees which have lifted him to the title of Indian Idol over Amit.
