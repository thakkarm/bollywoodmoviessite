10/30/2006
Australia beats India in the 12th ODI of the Champions Trophy by 6 wickets. The match was played in jab Cricket Association Stadium, Mohali - Chandigarh where India won the toss Elected To Bat. India scored 249 for 8 in 50 overs. Australia then batted to 252 for 4 in just over 45 overs to win the match. The Man of the Match was Damien Martyn for his batting performance scoring 73 runs which included 8 fours. For India V Sehwag scored 65 runs, R Dravid scored 52.
