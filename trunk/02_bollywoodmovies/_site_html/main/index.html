Amitabh Bachchan To Setup Girls College In Uttar Pradesh
Date: 04/24/2012:
Bollywood superstart Amitabh Bachchan will setup a girls college in Uttar Pradesh named after his daughter in law and Bollywood star Aishwarya Rai Bachchan . The Daulatpur village committee has handed over the land to Amitabh Bachchan Sewa Samiti (ABSS), a newly formed organisation and the construction will commence soon. The school will start by having classes for nursery to class 12 and then be upgraded to degree college. Bachchan had laid the foundation stone of Aishwarya Rai College in the Daulatpur village on January 27, 2008.

Sania and Anastasia captures Pattaya Open
02/13/2012:
Indian star Sania Mirza and her doubles Australian partner Anastasia Rodionova won the women's doubles final of the Pattaya Open tennis championship in Thailand. This happens to be the 13th WTA title for the Sania Mirza and the first for 2012. Sania is ranked World No 7 in women's double has a chance to compete in the 2012 London Olympics.
