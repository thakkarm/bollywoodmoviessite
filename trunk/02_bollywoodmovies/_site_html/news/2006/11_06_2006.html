Aishwarya Rai and Hrithik Roshan Off To Jodhpur
Date: 11/09/2006
Aishwarya Rai and Hrithik Roshan packed their bags and headed off to Jodhpur on jet plane to start to shoot Costume drama Jodha Akbar (2007) . Aish seems to star in many movies that required fancy costume from Chokher Bali to Devdas to Umrao Jaan to The Last Leagion and now Jodha Akbar. The movie is being directed by Ashutosh Gowariker who has previously directed Swades starring Shah Rukh Khan (SRK) and Lagaan staring Aamir Khan .
Neha Dhupia To Perform Item Song
Date: 11/08/2006
Neha Dhupia has been selected to perform a item song for the movie Jimmy And Johny . The movie stars Sanjay Dutt , Saif Ali Khan , Bipasha Basu and Kim Sharma . The movie is being produced by producer, Mahendra Dhariwal and directed by Ajay Chandhok. Neha last appeared in a item song and brief appearance in Garam Masala.
