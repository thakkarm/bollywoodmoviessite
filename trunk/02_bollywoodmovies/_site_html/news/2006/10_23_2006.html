Aishwarya Rai and Abhishek Bachchan to wed?
Date: 10/29/2006
Is a wedding in the planning for Aishwarya Rai and Abhishek Bachchan ? The rumor mills are going wild with the latest rumor that both families have consented to the wedding of the hottest pair in Bollywood. Aishwarya Rai , Bollywood leading lady and former Miss World, and Abhishek Bachchan have been working very closly in the last couple of months on Umrao Jaan , Guru and Dhoom 2 and that may have let the star pair to get closer. Both famlies believe in matching horoscopes and both stars are believed to be Mangliks. A wedding between two mangliks with matching horoscopes is considered very auspicious.
John and Bipasha To Star Together In Goal
Date: 10/23/2006
John Abraham and Bipasha Basu have signed up to star together in Vivek Agnihotri’s Goal (2007) . The pair are a off screen pair and last starred together in Jism and Madhoshi. In Goal Bipasha plays a Pakistani girl.

Mona Singh Win Jhalak Dikhhlaa Jaa Final
Date: 10/26/2006
The final of Jhalak Dikhhlaa Jaa was not much surprising. The final lastest more then 2 hours where all dance pairs came together and danced. In the end the winner was announced to be no other then the famous Jassi (Mona Singh). She has become so well known with the Indian public, due to the great success of her show Jassi Jassi Kou Nia, that could have led her to get such great public response.
Personnally I thought Shveta should have won, she danced great and she brought the sexyness that the songs demanded. Mona was a good dancer but I think between the two, Shveta was better and more smoother.
Sushmita Sen To Play Radha In Sholay
Date: 10/23/2006
Sushmita Sen to play the role of widow Radha in Ram Gopal Varma's Sholay (2006) until now everyone was under the impression that Sushmita would be playing the role of Basanti. She will be replacing the role that was going to be played by Katrina Kaif . Ram's explains that Radha in the new Sholay will be more practical.
