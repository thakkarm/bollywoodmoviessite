Bollywood News:
Rani Mukherjee In Controversial Situation On Set Of Chudiyan
Date: 11/15/2006
Rani Mukherjee is currently in Varanasi shooting for Yash Raj Film’s Chudiyan (2007) directed by Pradeep Sarkar where she got herself into some controversial situation when her bodyguards charged the media persons and also her fans. A political group has asked for her to apology in 48hrs or leave the city. She later did apologize to the media but also added that the media was trying to get too close to her and Konkana Sen Sharma.
Aishwarya Rai summoned by Customs Department
Date: 11/15/2006
Aishwarya Rai has been sent a summon by the Customs Department to appear at their office at Ballard Pier. The report is that a parcel from Netherlands has arrived that contains 24,000 euros, a DVD player, a Ralph Lauren Polo Sport T-shirt and pair of binoculars addressed to her. The Customs Department opened the parcel in front of witnesses once the actress failed to come to the office. Aishwarya Rai who is currently shooting for Ashutosh Gowarikar’s Jodha Akbar (2007) is not in Mumbai and will possibly return by this weekend.
Saif Ali Khan, Vidya Balan and Shiney Ahuja To Do A Short Film
Date: 11/13/2006
Saif Ali Khan , Vidya Balan and Shiney Ahuja will be starring in a short film on AIDS awareness to be directed by Mira Nair. This may be part of the of the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation Initiative in India to combat AIDS and create awareness.
