Finally Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Have Tied The Knot
Date: 04/20/2007:

The ceremony was small, but the bringing togetherness of the two by far was not. Both are well established actors in Bollywood, and Aish is well known worldwide. She has a wax statue of her in London's world famous Madame Tussaud’s Wax Museum next to her new father-in-law, Amitabh Bachchan, has won the Miss World crown in 1994, has a flower named after, has endorsements with many big companies like L'Oreal and has worked in many movies with varity of role and success. It is not to say that Abhishek is left behind, he also has found great success with his movies and is making a name for himself in Bollywood. He has to work very hard too fill the shoes of Bollywood's biggest star, his father Amitabh Bachchan .
Today will mark the beginning of a new era for the Bachchan family and for Bollywood movie industry. We all want to wish both Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai the best on their new adventure and wish both families many memorable moments in the future.

What is Bollywood?
Date: 04/20/2007:
Bollywood is used to reference the Indian cinema industry. The name is a combination of Bombay (now known as Mumbai) and Hollywood, American film industry. Mainly the movies from Bollywood are in Hindi and Urdu and does not include movies in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada etc. Bollywood is the largest procedure, in terms of numbers, of movies in the world and in number of tickets sold. Bollywood movies are world known and are watched throught the world.
