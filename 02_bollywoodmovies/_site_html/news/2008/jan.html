Aishwarya Rai Wax Statue In New York
Date: 08/08/2007:
Aishwarya Rai's wax statue will be added to the Madame Tussauds museum at Times Square, New York on August 15 which is the day India will celebrate it's 60th year of independence. The statue is borrowed from the London museum, which also has other was models of Bollywood star like Amitabh Bachchan , Shahrukh Khan (SRK) and Tamil superstar Rajnikanth. The Madame Tussauds museum has locations in London, Amsterdam, Las Vegas, Hong Kong, Shanghai and are soon opening one in Washington, DC.
