India News
10/06/2008:
Tata Motors announced that the new home of the Nano will be Sanand in Gujarat. Many local governments were interested in hosting the plant, but after reviewing the offer from the Gujarat Government it was decided that it offered the the best option for the project. The plant will require about 1100 acres and will produce 250,000 cars per annum and will be expanded up to 500,000 cars per annum. Until the Sanand plant’s completion, Tata Motors will have to look at existing facilities at Pune and Pantnagar to manufacture the Nano, and plans to launch the car in the last quarter of this financial year.

Money, Finance, Technology News:
10/30/2008:
Mitsubishi Digital Electronics announced the availability of the world's first, laser powered television. The Mitsubishi LaserVue 65 inch model will be sold for $6999 at specialty retailers across the US. The LaserVue line has been introduced as the most energy efficient large format, high definition television on the market today. LaserVue promises to provide two times the color of many of today's HDTVs and will use less power than LCD and plasma TVs. The LaserVue TV is designed for both wall mount as well as floor stand with a full stereo range of sound capabilities with integrated speakers. Mitsubishi L65A90 LaserVue is offered with a 65 inch screen diagonal with a 73 inch model to be coming later this year. The LaserVue works with a resolution of 1080p and is equipped with 4 HDMI connections.
