Katrina Kaif Become Sexiest Asian Woman
Date: 12/15/2009:
Katrina Kaif retained the top spot in an annual list of the world's 50 sexiest Asian women. The British born Bollywood actress beat out many to be number one. Indian actresses took 8 of the top 10 spots, with Laila Rouass, British television star, and Pakistani actress Mehwish Hayat completing the list. The top 10 of the Eastern Eye 50 Sexiest Asian Women were Katrina Kaif, Priyanka Chopra, Freida Pinto, Laila Rouass, Kareena Kapoor, Bipasha Basu, Lara Dutta, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan , Mehwish Hayat and Deepika Padukone.

Aishwarya Rai And Sir Ben Kingsley In Film Taj
Date: 12/03/2009:
British actor Sir Ben Kingsley well known for his Oscar winning portrayal of Indian independence hero Mohandas Gandhi, has signed former Miss World and Bollywood actress Aishwarya Rai to star opposite him in a movie Taj Mahal. Aishwarya will play the role of Queen Mumtaz and Ben Kingsley will play the role of her husband Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the movie titled, Taj (2010) . Kingsley’s wife, Brazilian actress Daniela Lavender will play the role of Persian Princess Kandahari Begum, Shah Jahan’s first wife.

World News:
05/18/2009:
The high priced golden culinary herb saffron, made from crocus flowers, and used in cooking, may hold one of the keys to preventing the loss of sight in old age and could even help improve vision in people suffering from certain blinding eye diseases. A team found that saffron slows the loss of sight becuase the herb has remarkable effects on the genes which actually regulate the performance of the eye's key vision cells.
