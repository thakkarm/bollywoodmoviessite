Bollywood News:
Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna Will Be Launch at I.I.F.A.
Date: 06/09/2006
The music for the most awaited movie of the year, Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna (KANK) , will be launched at the I.I.F.A. weekend, on June 16. Karan along with Amitabh Bachchan , Abhishek Bachchan and Rani Mukherjee will be present to launch the music. Rani Mukherjee is also in the news since she recently signed up as the brand ambassador for Titan Raga, a watch brand which is designed and crafted exclusively for women.
Priyanka Chopra and Akshaye Khanna In Aap Ki Khatir
Date: 06/05/2006
Priyanka Chopra and Akshaye Khanna will be seen in Aap Ki Khatir being directed by Dharmesh Darshan. The film is currently in post production and is scheduled for release in the next 2 months. This will mark the first time Priyanka Chopra and Akshaye Khanna has starred in a movie together and the rumors have it that they have a great sizzling chemistry on screen. Other stars include Amisha Patel , Dino Morea , Suniel Shetty and Anupam Kher and the music is being provided by the hottest music director Himesh Reshammiya .
Bipasha Basu To In Double Role
Date: 06/05/2006
Bipasha Basu for first time in her career will be playing double role in the Abbas-Mustan’s next untitled currently being shot in London. The comedy film also stars Ajay Devgan and Sanjay Dutt .

06/06/2006 IBM plans to triple investment in India
IBM plans to triple investment in India over the next three years to $6 billion to take advantage of the country's lower-cost labor and diverse engineering skills. The funding will also add new service delivery centers in Bangalore and a telecommunications research facility in New Delhi. IBM now employs more people in India than any other foreign company.
