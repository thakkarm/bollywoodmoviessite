What is Bollywood?
Date: 04/20/2007:
Bollywood is used to reference the Indian cinema industry. The name is a combination of Bombay (now known as Mumbai) and Hollywood, American film industry. Mainly the movies from Bollywood are in Hindi and Urdu and does not include movies in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada etc. Bollywood is the largest procedure, in terms of numbers, of movies in the world and in number of tickets sold. Bollywood movies are world known and are watched throught the world.
