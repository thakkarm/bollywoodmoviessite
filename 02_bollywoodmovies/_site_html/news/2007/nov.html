Abhishek and Aishwarya Bachchan Dance For A Cause
Date: 11/29/2007:
With World Aids Day on Dec 1, 2007, the Bollywood couple Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Bachchan recently were seen together spending time with children affected with HIV/AIDS as part of association called Ashray. Abhishek has been part of the Ashray Association since 2001.
Sanjay Dutt Granted Bail
Date: 11/27/2007:
Sanjay Dutt has been granted bail by the Supreme Court and should be released from the Pune jail within the next couple of days. Sanjay was given a six year jail term after being convicted in connection with the 1993 Mumbai serial blasts.
Saawariya Screening Held Oct 28
Date: 10/30/2007:
The first movie screening for Saanvariya, Saawariya (2006) was held Sunday night Oct 28, 2007. The movie received rave reviews and is set for a great opening when it is released next Friday. The movie is about 2 hours and 8 mins long and contains about 8 songs, which is make it a short film per Sanjay Leela Bhansali 's standards. The film stars two new comers, Ranbir Kapoor and Sonam Kapoor , from two of the biggest Bollywood families.

Tennis News
11/29/2007:
Indian Cricket League (ICL) will begin it ingural match tomorrow Nov 30, 2007. The match will be between Chandigarh Lions vs Delhi Jets and will follow the 2020 cricket format. The ICL is a newly formed cricket league in India, and is being backed by Zee Telefilms, Kapil Dev, and other great cricket players. The ICL is made up of 6 teams being Mumbai Champs, Chennai Superstars, Chandigarh Lions, Hyderabad Heroes, Kolkata Tigers, and Delhi Jets. The league includes such welknown players like Michael Bevan, Inzamam ul Haq, Darryl Cullinan, Craig McMillan, Madan Lal, Sandip Patil, Brian Lara and many more.
11/27/2007:
The Indian and Pakistani cricket team arrived in Kolkata for their next test match. The test match will be played from November 30 to December 4 at the Eden Gardens. India has already won the first test match by six wicket which was played at Ferozshah Kotla ground in New Delhi.
