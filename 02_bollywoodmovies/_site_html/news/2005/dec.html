Aish's lose is Rani's gain, Once again
Date: 12/29/2005
Rani Mukherjee , the dusky Bengal beauty, has once again replaced former Miss World Aishwarya Rai in a Bhansali movie. The movie, Saanvariya , is being directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, who also directed Black , in which Aishwarya was first offered the role but later was replaced by Rani. The movie Saanvariya, is also introduces two new stars, Anil Kapoor's daughter Sonam and Rishi Kapoor's son Ranbir . Sanjay wanted Aishwarya to play opposite Salman Khan in Saanvariya , but the relations between Salman and Aish continue to be too strained and since Sanjay prefers Salman for the male lead, and hence Aish had to opt out of the movie.
Gurinder Chadha and Farah Khan to appear on Koffee with Karan
Date: 12/28/2005
Koffee with Karan had director Gurinder Chadha and choreographer/director Farah Khan on the latest edition of his TV show.
Farah Khan is the first woman to be nominated for Best Director Filmfare Awards for her debut direction in Main Hoon Na starring Shahrukh Khan, Sushmita Sen, Zayed Khan, Sunil Shetty and Amrita Rao. She is notably for her choreography and is regarded as the hottest choreographer in Bollywood. She is also one of three judges for Sony's TV Show, Indian Idol , with Sonu Nigam and Anu Malik. Farah's more famous choreographer sequence are hit movies like Dil Se, Kuch Kuch Hota Hai, Kabhi Kushi Kabhie Gham, and Mohabbatein. She has also being nominated for Broadway’s 2004 Tony Award as Best Choreographer, along with collaborator Anthony Van Laast for Bombay Dreams.
Gurinder Chadha became famous internationally for her direction of Bend it Like Beckham, which she also co-authored. She then directed Aishwarya Rai and Martin Henderson in Bride and Prejudice. Her other films include Bhaji on the Beach (1994) and What’s Cooking? (2000). Born in Kenya and brought up in London, she began her career as a news reporter with BBC Radio.

Does Aishwarya Rai need to worry?
Date: 12/14/2005
While Aishwarya Rai’s over-committed date schedule is proving to be a blessing in disguise for Priyanka Chopra who has taken her place in a good number of films. In fact, aish has got a record number of period films lined up from Jodha Akbar to Umrao Jaan and Ketan Mehta’s Jhansi Ki Rani. There’s YashRaj Films’ Dhoom II, two other films lined up with Ratan Jain of Venus, besides one with Mani Ratnam. However he admits that she was a bit put off with her experience with the makers of Bluff Master , when Priyanka Chopra took her place in the film without her knowledge. Sholay's remake has Katrina playing Jaya role.
Remake of 'Sholay' by Ram Gopal Verma has planned to use alman Khan's love interest Katrina Kaif. Highly appealing Katrina who performed a small role in Ramu's 'Sarkaar' would be playing the Jaya Bachchan's role in this remake. Ramu has also signed the 'Rangeela' girl Urmila Matondkar to dance on the remix version of "Mahbooba Mahbboba".
Bollywood movie actress Aishwarya Rai says finding work in Hollywood is not an easy task. The former Miss India, long a major box office draw in India, recently wrapped up filming “The Last Legion,” a Hollywood epic about a Roman warrior, played by Colin Firth, who must protect a young Caesar as the Roman Empire crumbles. Shot in Slovakia, “The Last Legion” is being produced by Dino De Laurentiis and also stars Ben Kingsley. It is scheduled to be released next year.
After three long years of courtship, rumours are that Aishwarya Rai and Vivek Oberoi have decided to call it a day and tread their separate paths. Ash and Vivek were last seen together at a friend’s birthday celebration in August. Viveik, who seldom left any opportunity to proclaim his love for the most beautiful lady on the earth, is apparently heartbroken. After her return, the actress first plunged into the shooting of Dhoom 2, and has since been busy with the shooting of J P Dutta’s Umrao Jaan in Jaipur. Viveik, meanwhile, has been doing his best to side-step questions about Aishwarya. Another cause of the split was the dreaded S-word — shaadi. The Rais were getting impatient, but Vivek (29), who’s younger than Ash (32), wanted to concentrate solely on his career. In the end, there were too many issues and no solution in sight. The couple took a mutual decision to separate.
