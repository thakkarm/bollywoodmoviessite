Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa, dancing with the stars, is a celebrities dancing show that pairs a welcome celebrities with a professional dance partners to perform together. The celebrities are choosen from different walks of life such as sports, films, fashion, etc. The show is based on the concept by BBC and has been a huge success in countries like Britain, America, Australia, New Zealand and Italy. The panel of three judges consists Sanjay Leela Bhansali, Farha Khan and Shilpa Shetty. The show is hosted by Archana Puran Singh & Parmeet Sethi.

Date: 10/26/2006
The final of Jhalak Dikhhlaa Jaa was not much surprising. The final lastest more then 2 hours where all dance pairs came together and danced. In the end the winner was announced to be no other then the famous Jassi (Mona Singh). She has become so well known with the Indian public, due to the great success of her show Jassi Jassi Kou Nia, that could have led her to get such great public response.
Personnally I thought Shveta should have won, she danced great and she brought the sexyness that the songs demanded. Mona was a good dancer but I think between the two, Shveta was better and more smoother.
