The Judges
In 2004-2005, the 1st edition of the popular Indian idol contest was judged by of Sonu Nigam, Anu Malik and Farah Khan. They again judged the next set of contestants for year 2005-2006, Indian Idol 2 but for season 3 of Indian Idol two new judges will join Anu Malik, and they are Udit Narayan and Alisha Chinoy.
Udit Narayan
Udit Narayan is playback singer that is well know in Bollywood. He has sung for many movies and has received many awards from the movie industry. Some of his well know songs include Papa Kehte Hain, Mera Dil Tere Liye, Pehla Nasha, Jadu Teri Nazar, Mehndi Lagake Rakhna, Kya Kare Kya Na Kare, Pardesi Pardesi, Dil To Pagal Hai, Kuchh Kuchh Hota Hai, Koi Mil Gaya, Kaho Na Pyar Hai, Mitwaa, Main Yahan Hoon, Yeh Tara Woh Tara and Khaike paan banaraswala to mention a few.
Anu Malik
Anu is a well known music director in the Bollywood film industry. He has won many awards including the National Award for his work in Refugee.
Alisha Chinoy
Alisha Chinoy is an Indian singer and movie actress. She started her career as a pop singer and met with good success initially but then she has some less successful releases. She has since comeback to films as a playback singer. She is well known for her recent successes like Kajra Re, Ishq Di Gali, Aaj Ki Raat and Touch me.

03/06/2007:
Season 3 of the Indian Idol will bring on 2 new judges to replace Sonu Nigam and Farah Khan. The new judge, Alisha Chinoy and Udit Narayan, will join Anu Malik for season 3 of Indian Idol. Auditions dates: Jodhpur & Bhubaneshwar (Feb 15th), Hyderabad & Amritsar (Feb 18th), Srinagar & Nagpur (Feb 21st), Baroda (Feb 27th & 28th), Bhopal (March 7th & 8th), Kanpur (March 12th & 13th), Delhi (March 17th, 18th & 19th), Kolkata (March 24th, 25th & 26th), and Mumbai (March 31st , 1st & 2nd April).
02/03/2007
Both Sonu Nigam and director-choreographer Farah Khan will not be returning as celebrity judge from Indian Idol 3 .
