10/5/2010:
India beats Australia in Mohali in an unbelievable finish. India wins a one wicket victory against Australia, in the first of a two-test series. VVS Laxman set up a decisive 81 run ninth wicket partnership with Ishant Sharma, India came back from 124/8 to stun the Australian. On the fifth and final day of the first test at Mohali, Laxman's 73 runs from 79 balls ensured India will retain the Border Gavaskar Trophy.
