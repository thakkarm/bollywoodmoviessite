Abhishek and Aishwarya To Wed On April 19

Shilpa Shetty Meets Queen Elizabeth II
Date: 03/15/2007:

India beats Bermuda by a whopping 257 runs. India scored a world cup record 413/5 in 50.0 overs while Bermuda was bowled out for just 156 in just under 43 overs. India came back stronge from the lost to Bangladesh and now must win against Sri Lanka to make it into the next round. For India V. Sehwag scored 114 runs which included 17 fours and 3 sixes, S. Ganguly again played well with 89 runs. Y. Singh put together a quick 83 runs with 4 fours and 7 sixes, S. Tendulkar added 57 runs from 29 balls with 2 fours and 4 sixes. For Bermuda D. Hemp scored 76 and remind not out. The Indan bowlers had a great outting bowling out Bermuda with A. Kumble taking 3 wickets.
