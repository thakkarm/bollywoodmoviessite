Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa Latest News and Headlines:
Akshay Kumar and Katrina Kaif To Be Guest On Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa Finals
Date: 12/14/2007:
Akshay Kumar and Katrina Kaif will be guest stars on the Sony dance show Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa during the final to arrive December 20, 2007. The Bollywood stars are going to promote their upcoming movie, Welcome, releasing on December 21, 2007.
09/23/2007:
Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 2 is all set of a all new season. The show is to start to air from September 28, 2007 on Sony Entertainment Television. This year the show will have three new judges, to include Urmila Matondkar , Shiamak Davar, and Jeetendra. The show will feature 12 stars being trained by Choreographer to dance away and win the audience heart to be crowned the Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 2 winner.
