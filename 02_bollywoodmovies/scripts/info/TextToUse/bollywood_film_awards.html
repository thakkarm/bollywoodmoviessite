Zee Cine Awards will celebrate its 10th anniversary next year and the venue has been decided to be Bangkok. The awards will take place on 3rd March, 2007 at Impact Arena, Bangkok. Rang De Basanti is going to be watched by the 95 member team (Hollywood Foreign Press Association) the official body for Golden Globe Awards. The Hollywood Foreign Press Association screens movies before arriving at the nominations decision and announcement towards the end of the year. Rang De Basanti was directed by Rakesh Mehra and stars Aamir Khan and Soha Ali Khan .

IIFA 2007 To Be Held In Yorkshire
Date: 06/04/2007:
This year the IIFA 2007 awards will be held at Yorkshire on June 09, 2007. The award will feature many awards and many dances by top Bollywood movie stars. The IIFA award will be hosted by Boman Irani and Lara Dutta with special awards section will be done by Karan Johar . Scheduled performance include Abhishek Bachchan , Salman Khan , Akshay Kumar, Emraan Hashmi, Aftab Shivdasani, Saif Ali Khan , Shilpa Shetty , Bipasha Basu and Raima Sen .

Bollywood Movie News:
06/16/2006
International Indian Film Academy (IIFA) event has officially begun in Dubai from 14 June and will continue till 17 June. IIFA awards which are deemed as the Indian equivalent of Oscars will showcase 32 Indian feature films including sixteen Hindi films, fourteen south Indian films, one Bengali, one Marathi and six short films. The IIFA awards were started back in 2000 and it was first held in London. Since then it has been held twice in South Africa and once in Malaysia, Singapore and the Netherlands and now in Dubai.
